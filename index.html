WELCOME TO Dr. D.Y.PATIL COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY KASABA BAWADA, KOLHAPUR

Established in 1984, DY Patil College of Engineering and Technology (also known as DYPCOET) is one of the premier Engineering colleges located in Kolhapur, Maharashtra. The College comes under the purview of Dr DY Patil Technical Campus (DYPTC) founded by the chairman of DY Patil Group honorable Dr Ajeenkye DY Patil. The College has been approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the Directorate of Technical Education Government of Maharashtra (DTE GoM). Also, it is affiliated to Shivaji University Kolhapur and is accredited with 'A' grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).The campus of DYPCOET is spread across 205 acre of pollution-free infrastructure and includes facilities such as state-of-the-art buildings, auditorium, sports complex, seminar halls, mess, food joints, bus facilities, 24*7 medical assistance and more. The College has a central library which is established with a stack room as well as a reading room with sufficient area as instructed by the AICTE. With around 2,893 titles and 14,961 volumes , DYPCOET has access to OPAC (Online Public Access Catalog) through LAN and a digital Library with 27 personal computers available for all the students and staff. The College has partnered with several giant incubation and assessment centres such as TATA Technologies, Hortonworks University, My Anatomy, Pariksha.Co, Autodesk, etc. as well. Every Engineering department at DYPCOET inculcate distinct course-related laboratories that help students acquire practical learning and exposure . As of now, 69 companies participated in the drive and a total of 271 students have been placed. CSE emerged as the top branch with 157 students placed so far. The highest and average packages stood at INR 10 LPA and INR 6.9 LPA, respectively.

Dr. DY Patil College of Engineering and Technology Kolhapur offers BTech in five specialisations, namely Civil, Computer, Electronics & Telecommunication, Mechanical and Chemical, and BArch at UG level. The duration of full-time BTech is four years, whereas BArch is offered with five-year duration. At PG level, the College offers MTech in two specialisations, namely Computer Science and Electronics & Telecommunication. The duration of MTech is two years. Other than these, DY Patil College of Engineering and Technology offers BTech (lateral entry) in all the BTech specialisations.
